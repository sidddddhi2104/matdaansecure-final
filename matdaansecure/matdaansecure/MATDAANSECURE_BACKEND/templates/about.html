About MatdaanSecure

MatdaanSecure is a revolutionary digital platform designed to simplify, secure, and strengthen the democratic process. It enables eligible Indian voters to cast their votes safely from anywhere, ensuring accessibility, transparency, and efficiency for every citizen.

🗳️ Voter's Guidelines

✅ What to Do

  • Verify voter ID and phone number before login.
  • Ensure you are on a secure private network.
  • Review candidate profiles carefully.

❌ What to Avoid

  • Never share OTP or login credentials.
  • Do not attempt to vote multiple times.
  • Ignore suspicious links or verification requests.
